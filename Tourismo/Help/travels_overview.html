Client Travels overview

In travels overview you are provided with the list of available travels. You are able to search travels by their name, and also filter travels by their price and available date range. Also you can sort travels by some of the given categories. When you want to have a look at all travels again, just press the reset button and you are good to go.

When you want to make a reservation click on the card of the travel which you want to go to and it will lead you to
Make a reservation page
