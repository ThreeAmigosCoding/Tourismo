Accommodation details

In this window, you can see and eddit accommodation details

You can change accommodation data in the appropriate inputs, and if you mess up, an error message will be shown here:

On the right side you have access to the map of Serbia. By double clicking anywhere on the map, the address will be updated automaticly. You can also enter an address first and click on the Map view button, wich will then pin the location on the map for you to verify it:

When you are done with changeing the data, you can save your work by clicking the Save button, or you can tap "Ctrl" + "S" on the keyboard.

If you want to delete the accommodation, you can click the Delete button, or tap "Ctrl" + "D" on the keyboard.

If you change your mind and want to get back to the Accommodation overview you can do so by tapping "Esc" on your keyboard.
