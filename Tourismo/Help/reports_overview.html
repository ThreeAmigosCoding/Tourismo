Agent Travel Reports overview

In travel reports overview you are provided with the list of travels that were sold in the month that you choose.

There's a month and year picker available at the top right corner.

You can see a summary which shows how many travels were sold for the period and how much money was earned.

When you want to see sold arrangements for certain travel, click on the button "Arrangements" in the bottom right corner of that travel card. It will lead you to Arrangements reports page.
