Agent Attractions overview

In attractions overview you are provided with the list of available attractions.

There's a search bar available at the top right corner so you can easily find attractions that you want.

When you want to add new attraction, click on the button at the bottom of the page.

When you want to make changes for an attraction, click on the card of the attraction which you want to go to and it will lead you to
Attraction details page.

When you want to delete attraction, click on the red button with a trash can in the bottom right corner of the attraction card. Additionaly, you can delete the attraction from the Attraction details page.
