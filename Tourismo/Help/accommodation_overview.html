Accommodation overview

In accommodations overview you are provided with the list of available accommodations.

There's a search bar available at the top right corner so you can easily find accommodations that you want.

If you want to add a new accommodation, you can do so by clicking the "Add new" button at the bottom of the page, or you can tap "Ctrl" + "N" on the keyboard, wich will take you to the Accommodation creation page

When you want to see and eddit accommodation details, you can click on the given accommodation wich will take you the Accommodation details page

Additionally, you can delete an accommodation directly from this window by clicking on the trash-bin icon at the bottom right of every accommodation in the list.
